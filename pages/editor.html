Quick Start
1 Load
2 Mark
3 Adjust
4 Check
5 Submit

1. Select a car from the database → auto-loads real dimensions, weight & physics.

2. Pick a 3D model from Pending Assets or Sketchfab panel, or drag & drop a GLB file onto the viewport.

The model auto-scales to match the selected car's real size. Attribution auto-fills from Sketchfab metadata.

Place wheel markers — press R, then click each wheel center on the mesh. Select which marker in the marker list first, or it auto-picks the next unplaced one.

Assign object marks — press A to open the component picker, then left-click meshes to mark (headlights, taillights, brake discs). Middle-click to remove a mark.

Auto-detect — press Shift+A to auto-find wheels and lights by name/geometry.

Scale — use scale controls to match the green reference prism to the car's real proportions.

Move markers — press W, select a marker, drag it. Paired markers (FL/FR) move in sync when locked.

Physics — click the Physics button to tweak mass, suspension, damping. "Reset Defaults" restores the selected car's values.

Validation panel — check that all required markers are placed and all errors are resolved.

Objects panel — verify badges (wheel, headlight, taillight, brake disc) are on the correct meshes.

Attribution — confirm the attribution field in the sidebar is filled (auto-populated from Sketchfab).

Hit Bake & Submit in the sidebar → exports GLB with markers baked in, uploads to S3, saves car config. Done!

Shortcuts: Q orbit · Shift+Q select · R place marker · A assign · W move · E explode · X wireframe · H highlights · 0 fit · Esc cancel
